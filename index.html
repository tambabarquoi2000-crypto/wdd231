About Me

My name is Tamba Barquoi. I live with my family in Paynesville, Liberia. My passion has always been computer science but when I was coming up I never relized it. Growing up I was always looked up to when people have problem with their phones and computers, eventhough I was not computer literate by then. During my secondary graduation I was asked what I would love to do in the future and I siad Petroleum Engineering but to be honest I never knew what it was. After our graduation I never had the opportunity to attend the State University, University of Liberia. I was then enrolled to a vocational computer institution that was run by a friend of my father. It was then I discovered that I am actually a computer enthusiast and started to pursue it as a career. After I graduated my mother wanted me to attend the state university at all cost so she insisted. Sincerely speaking, I never had passion for all their top priority courses but being a child took advantage of me. I started the university majoring Mathematics and minoring Economics. After two sucessful semester an opportunity came for me to work as a Data Administrator with a contractor company, ODL working for Arcelormittal Liberia. After the contract terminated I met a friend who introdued me to the BYU-Pathway program and as it stands it is serving my best dream ever. So, that's it, a little about me.
