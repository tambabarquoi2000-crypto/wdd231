Subject: Buy & Sell LIB

Purpose

Buy & Sell Liberia is a national ecommerce market place where both used and new items can be posted for review and purchase base on customer experience. It is going to have features like: posting, liking, reviewing, commenting and connection to whatsapp account of sellers.

Scenarios

  1. How do we know that each dealer is a verified and it is not a scam?
  2. What happens if I am not able to get my purchased item?
